About

This site analyzes the music you've recently listened to on Spotify, compares it to our database to find others with similar music taste, and suggests music based on what they have listened to. All you have to do is click the sign in button, and songs will begin to be recommended. Based on the nature of the site, the results become more accurate and unique as the number of users increases, so invite your friends!

Privacy

All data used by this site is anonymous. Your name, email, etc. will never be saved/utilized by us. All that we save is your recently played songs, and an anonymous user ID.
